# Shadow Lens Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026
**Last Updated:** June 2026

## Overview

Shadow Lens is a local-first AR sun & shade planner that respects your privacy. This app processes all data locally on your device without sending information to external servers.

## Data Collection & Processing

### Camera & AR Data
- Camera access is required to display AR content and detect horizontal surfaces
- **All camera data is processed locally** on your device in real-time
- Camera frames are NOT recorded, saved, or transmitted
- AR scene detection uses ARKit's on-device processing only

### Location Data
- **NOT collected or used** by Shadow Lens
- Sun calculations use your manual date/time input, not location services
- The app runs completely offline

### Device Motion Data
- Used locally for AR tracking (accelerometer, gyroscope)
- Processed on-device only via ARKit
- NOT transmitted or stored

### User-Created Data
- Your shadow calculations and placement data exist only on your device
- NOT synchronized to cloud services
- NOT shared with third parties

## Data Storage

- All shadow positions, object placements, and calculation history are stored locally
- No account login required
- No cloud synchronization
- Data persists only within the app on your device
- Uninstalling the app removes all stored data

## Third-Party Services

Shadow Lens does NOT use:
- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
- Crash reporting services (Sentry, Firebase Crashlytics)
- Advertisement networks
- Social media SDKs
- Location services
- Cloud storage or synchronization

## Permissions Explanation

**Camera Permission**
- Required to display AR overlay and detect ground planes
- Used exclusively for real-time AR processing on your device

**Motion Permission**
- Required for accurate AR head tracking and orientation
- Used by ARKit for on-device processing only

## Children's Privacy

Shadow Lens does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app processes everything locally with no external data transmission, there are no child-specific privacy concerns.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of Shadow Lens constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
